Published Unkown Astroid Data

In Spring 2023, I worked with a team of fellow students to discover the rotation period of asteroid 3214 Makarenko. Over the course of the semester we prepared to observe Makarenko over a 2 week period in May. During observation nights, we'd stay up until 7am observing this asteroid. At the end of the semseter, we published our findings in the Minor Planet Bulletin for anyone to access.
